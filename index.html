About Wong's

Wong's Chinese Restaurant boasts some of the most authentic, tasteful, and diligent expertise in the central Australian Region. Feel free to explore our digital menu of traditional Chinese delicacies. Wong’s Chinese Restaurant, a unique establishment nestled in the heart of Central Australia, presents itself as a cultural fusion like no other. Its online presence mirrors the idiosyncratic charm of the venue, with a website that combines vintage aesthetics and playful irony. The homepage greets visitors with bold red and gold accents, a nod to traditional Chinese motifs, while showcasing a menu that humorously defies expectations—kangaroo dishes and classic fish and chips feature prominently. Visitors are treated to quirky anecdotes about the restaurant’s origins and its enigmatic proprietor, Wong, who turned a run down shack into a 3 Michelin Star fine dining experience.
